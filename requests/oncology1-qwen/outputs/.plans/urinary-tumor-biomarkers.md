## Key Claims to Make
- Urinary tumor biomarkers represent a rapidly evolving field with significant potential for non-invasive cancer diagnosis
- Multiple categories exist: proteins, nucleic acids, EVs, and metabolites
- Performance varies significantly across different biomarker types and detection methods
- Clinical adoption is still in early stages but shows promise for reducing invasive procedures
