## Key Takeaway

EIS is essential for Jimini's analyte panel — it fills the gap where optics fails:
- **Creatinine**: enables dilution normalization (ACR, PCR ratios)
- **Glucose**: GOx enzyme electrode well-established
- **Ionic strength / SG**: low-frequency conductivity = osmolality surrogate
- **Albumin**: immunosensor for microalbuminuria
- **Bacteria**: label-free impedimetric detection

**EIS + optics fusion is unpublished** — this is Jimini's novel research opportunity.
